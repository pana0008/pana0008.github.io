Why this, why there, why, why?

Moving and studying in a foreign country has always been a dream of mine ever since I was little, which was really suprising for someone who has always been socially anxious. But I knew that this experience would break me out of my shell and help me grow into an independent and responsible adult.

It wasn't always clear to me what I wanted to study, because I had so many things that I was interested in and wanted to learn more about, but I knew I had to choose only one as my future career. And since I was always on the computer one of my choices was something with that! To be more specific I wanted to study game development and I started getting into it in 4th grade but I was really overwhelmed by it and didn't even know where to start so I didn't even try learning something about it because I thought that I wasn't smart enough for it.
