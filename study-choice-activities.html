Why this, why there, why, why?

Moving and studying in a foreign country has always been a dream of mine ever since I was little, which was really suprising for someone who has always been socially anxious. But I knew that this experience would break me out of my shell and help me grow into an independent and responsible adult.

It wasn't always clear to me what I wanted to study, because I had so many things that I was interested in and wanted to learn more about, but I knew I had to choose only one as my future career. And since I was always on the computer one of my choices was something with that! To be more specific I wanted to study game development and I started getting into it in 4th grade but I was really overwhelmed by it and didn't even know where to start so I didn't even try learning something about it because I thought that I wasn't smart enough for it.

So for a few years I had no idea where or what I was going to study. But during an Erasmus project in Zarazoga, Spain in the summer of 2022 I discovered that programming is not so scary after all. We only learnt a little bit of HTML and CSS but it was so fun. So I decided to sign up for a Python Basics course in the autumn and there began my coding journey.

During that same time I went to an event organized by a company called UNIFY, that helps future students from Bulgaria with applying to a university in the Netherlands. I was fascinated by all the different universities and different programmes that this country had to offer but I was most interested in HZ's ICT programme, because it covers the different languages and aspects of programming So that's how I made my choice and spent the last two years of high school keeping my grades up, practicing my English and preparing myself so I could apply to HZ University.

After completing the study

After graduating I really hope to have built a strong foundation in programming. Even if it isn't immediately I really hope that someday I am able to find a job as a game developer, because that has always been my dream field.
