Recieving feedback is important for personal and professional growth, improvement and maintaining healthy communication.

The first official feedback I recieved at HZ was from the Program and Career Orientation Website Pitch Day. I got feedback from a teacher, a teacher assistant and three students. Everyone gave me such wonderful and helpful suggestions and criticism on how to improve the design of my website and the formatting of my code, as well as really nice comments especially about my choice of theme and my poster! I recieved a really nice suggestion to add a return button to each blog page that leads to the main blog page and to put my images in a seperate folder to make things more organized.

I was recieving feedback way before that too, because I gathered with fellow students in the study rooms so we could motivate and help eachother with our websites.
