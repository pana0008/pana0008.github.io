Strengths

  • always motivated and determined to learn more
  • good time management
  • patient

Ever since I was little I have been always curious about everything, always hungry for knowledge which I would say is one of my biggest positive qualities. I am also really good at managing my time and organizing my tasks, I usually write down everything in my bullet journal. Patience is one of my strengths too and I think it's useful especially when working in teams.

Weaknesses

  • overworking myself to the point of exhaustion
  • really anxious when presenting

Even though it's good to be motivated to learn and do a lot of things I tend to lose control when doing that and I often overwork myself to the point where I could straight up pass out on my desk.

Opportunites

  • internship programs
  • development of new skills

One of the biggest opportunites I will have at HZ and overall during the course of my life is the development of new skills - both professional and personal.

Threats

  • risk of burnout
  • anxiety

I have struggled with anxiety for the entirety of my life and even though I have learned how to take control over it, there are times when it sadly takes control over me.
